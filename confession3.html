Confession 3 💌

Tumi amar jibon er shobcheye boro shopno. Choto boro shobno, shob kichu ami tumi’r sathe pura korte chai. 💫
Tumi jokhon kotha bolo, amar mon ekdom shanti pai. ❤️ Tumi amar shobcheye boro asha, amar shob kichu tumi’r sathe shuru korte chai. 🌸
Ami tumi’r sathe chole jete chai, je kono dike, je kono somoy, bas tumi thako amar kache. 🌹
Tumi chara kichu hoy na, tumi amar sabkichu. 😘
Tumi ki amay, amar motoi bhalobasho? Naki tar cheyeo beshi? ❤️
